WHERE LAVISHNESS AND INNOVATION CONVERGE.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Applaud 38 is a thoughtfully crafted address in the heart of Goregaon, where quiet haven meets contemporary living. Set between the serenity of Aarey's green expanse and the energy of the city, it offers a rare balance, calm within, connectivity beyond.

Designed with a focus on space, light, and everyday comfort, Applaud brings together refined design and considered amenities to create a lifestyle that feels elevated, composed, and enduring.

A LOCATION THAT
LIVES IN BALANCE

Goregaon sits where nature and the city find common ground. From lush, tree-covered pockets of Aarey to the connected world of metro and rail, infrastructure, and arterial roads, the location is both grounded and connected to Mumbai.

With proximity to commercial hubs, lifestyle centres, and JVLR connectivity, Goregaon is not just a location. It is a way of life where progress and peace exist side by side, and everything essential is within reach.
