OUR IMPACT

At IM Buildcon, our commitment goes beyond building spaces we invest in
building lives and communities. Through our initiatives in healthcare and
education, we actively contribute to social infrastructure
that creates lasting impact.

IM MULTISPECIALITY HOSPITAL

IM Multispeciality Hospital is one of the leading
healthcare centres in Vasai delivering high
quality medical care across more than
20 specialisations with the support of over 100
qualified doctors, setting strong benchmarks in
accessible and reliable healthcare.

IM SHIKSHAN SANSTHA

IM Shikshan Sanstha extends our impact to
education, nurturing young minds through a well
established school in Miraj, Maharashtra.
By focusing on foundational learning, the institution
continues to shape future generations
and strengthen society at its roots.

At IM Buildcon,
we believe real estate is more than infrastructure, it is responsibility.
Nestled between vibrant urban metros and serene forest landscapes,
our developments are thoughtfully designed to create harmony
between progress and preservation.

Our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives reflect this philosophy:
sustainable growth that uplifts communities while safeguarding the environment.
